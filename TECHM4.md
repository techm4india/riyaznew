# About TechM4Solutions

TechM4Solutions (TechM4India) is a deep-tech innovation company founded
to build affordable, practical, AI-powered SaaS products that eliminate
the chronic losses plaguing India\'s 63 million MSMEs --- and export
these solutions worldwide. Our 84-product innovation pipeline covers all
6 MSME Hackathon 6.0 themes.

## The 3 Laws of TechM4 Products

1.  **Fix a Real Loss:** Every product must reduce organisational loss
    by 30--50%+. Not a nice-to-have --- a measurable rupee-denominated
    loss the customer already knows about.

2.  **India-First, Export-Ready:** Designed for Indian MSME reality
    (₹2--15L budgets, ITI-trained operators, low connectivity) and
    simultaneously certified for USA, EU, Germany, Japan, Africa, and
    Gulf markets.

3.  **SaaS + IoT Flywheel:** Hardware is the hook; SaaS is the moat.
    Every product generates recurring monthly revenue through cloud
    dashboards, compliance reports, ML model updates, and API access.
