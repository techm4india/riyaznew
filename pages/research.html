Six Deep-Tech Labs

Research Infrastructure Built for Breakthroughs

Each lab is equipped with industry-grade tools, curriculum-mapped to engineering programs, and staffed by mentors from HCL, JPMorgan, Deloitte, and Tech Mahindra.

AI & ML Lab

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

The flagship lab. GPU-equipped workstations running real model training. Students build on TechM4Solutions' deployed AI products — AirGuard Pro, FoodSpectra, and ColdChainEye. Research focus: recommendation systems, NLP for Indian languages, and computer vision for agriculture.

FinTech Lab

Financial Technology Research

India's payments revolution is still unfinished. Students in this lab build credit scoring models for underserved populations, explore UPI-layer applications, and research RegTech automation. Connected to JPMorgan Chase's technology team for live problem briefs each semester.

IoT & Robotics Lab

Internet of Things & Robotics

From smart agriculture sensors deployed in Telangana farms to autonomous campus robots — this lab bridges hardware and software. Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and custom PCB fabrication available. Research in precision agriculture, industrial automation, and smart campus systems.

BioTech Lab

Biotechnology & Bioinformatics

The convergence of biology and computation. Students work on genetic data analysis, biosensor prototyping, and health diagnostics using AI. Research partners include diagnostics startups in Hyderabad's biotech corridor. Focus: affordable diagnostics for rural India.

Active Research Domains

What We're Working On Right Now

Ongoing research projects across labs, with real milestones and publication targets. Every project has a student researcher as primary investigator.

🌾

AI for Smart Agriculture

NLP-based advisory systems for Telangana farmers, integrated with FoodSpectra. Real-time soil moisture prediction using IoT sensors and satellite imagery. Target: 10,000 farmers by 2026.

🏙️

Smart Campus Systems

Automated attendance, energy monitoring, predictive maintenance, and student safety systems. Deployed across partner institutions. SchoolOS is built on this research foundation.

⚡

Energy Efficiency IoT

AirGuard Pro product research — affordable signal and leak detection for manufacturing plants in India who seek to minimize energy waste. Anomaly detection using edge AI classifiers.

🏥

Rural Health Diagnostics AI

Computer vision models for low-cost diagnostic imaging, trained on Indian patient data. Designed for ASHA workers and primary health centres in Telangana's rural districts.

🚗

Future Mobility & EV Tech

Battery management system optimisation, EV charging network planning for Tier-2 Indian cities, and smart traffic AI. Collaboration with Tech Mahindra's automotive division.

🌐

NLP for Indian Languages

Telugu, Hindi, and Tamil language models for educational content generation, voice interfaces, and accessibility. Training on curated regional datasets. Open-source first approach.

☁️

Cloud-Native Education Infrastructure

Multi-tenant LMS architecture designed for Indian bandwidth constraints. Edge-cached content delivery, offline-first mobile apps, and progressive web apps for 2G connectivity.

🤖

Autonomous Systems & Robotics

Warehouse automation prototypes, surgical robotics assistance algorithms, and agricultural drones with onboard AI. Tested in TechM4Engineering's IoT & Robotics lab environment.

TechM4Space

India's Next Frontier
Is Already Here

TechM4India is the only EdTech company in India with an active aerospace research program. We build, test, and fly. Students aren't just learning about space — they're contributing to India's next generation of aerospace engineers.

🛰️

CanSat Program

Full mission design cycle: payload specification, satellite miniaturisation, telemetry systems, and recovery operations. Students compete at national CanSat competitions.

🚁

Drone Engineering

Fixed-wing and multi-rotor design, autonomous flight path planning, FPV systems, and agricultural drone payload deployment. DGCA compliance training included.

🔥

Propulsion Systems

Solid-fuel and hybrid propulsion design for sounding rockets. Aerodynamic modelling, thrust curve analysis, and safe test-firing protocols. ISRO-inspired curriculum.

🌍

SpaceData Analytics

Remote sensing data processing, satellite imagery analysis for agriculture and disaster response, and orbit determination algorithms.

The TechM4India Research Pipeline

Every research project follows a structured journey from student-identified problem to deployed solution. No shelf research here.

💡

Problem Identification

Student + mentor co-define a real-world problem from industry or community

🔍

Literature & Lab Research

Structured review, lab-based experiments, prototype iteration in 6-week sprints

🛠️

Build & Test

Working prototype or model built in-lab, tested against real data or hardware

📋

Industry Review

MoU partner companies review and validate the solution against real use cases

🚀

Deploy or Publish

Top projects deployed in TechM4Solutions products or submitted for academic publication
