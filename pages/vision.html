100 Partner Institutions

From one university partnership to a hundred — every state in India with at least one TechM4India-equipped institution. The pipeline from school to startup, working at national scale.

TechM4Solutions' AI agricultural advisory reaching 100,000 farmers across Telangana — connecting student-built technology to the ground reality of Indian agriculture.

A network of TechM4India-powered smart campuses across India — AI-managed energy systems, automated attendance, predictive maintenance, and real-time student outcomes tracking built on our platform.

Global Innovation Partnerships

TechM4India's research output and student talent pipeline connected to universities and innovation hubs in Singapore, UK, and the US. Indian students competing and winning on the global stage.

100 partner institutions across 10 Indian states. Dedicated AI research centre launched in Hyderabad. First batch of 10,000 LSEP graduates placed. TechM4Schools in every Telangana district.

National Innovation Hub Network

1 million students touched annually. Smart campus network live. Indian students co-authoring research with global universities. TechM4India-built products used by farmers, hospitals, businesses, and governments across India.
