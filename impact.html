Company Milestones

From One Founder's Vision To a National Platform

The TechM4India story is still early. But every milestone below represents something real — a school visited, a lab installed, a company signed, a student changed.

2021

Ground Truth — 500+ Schools Visited

Mahi begins personally visiting schools across Telangana's 7 districts. Not surveys — actual conversations with students, teachers, and principals. The real problem becomes clear: no infrastructure, no industry connection, no future-readiness.

2022

TechM4India Founded · T-Hub Incubation

Company incorporated. Accepted into T-Hub — India's No.1 startup incubator backed by TGIIC. First lab format designed based on two years of field research. No templates. Built from scratch for the Indian context.

2023

First MoU Partners · NSDC Alignment

HCL Technologies and Tech Mahindra sign the first active MoUs — not logos, but live project brief commitments. N-STEP aligned with National Skill Development Corporation. Government-recognised certification pipeline established.

2024

JPMorgan · Deloitte · TGIIC Technology Partnership

JPMorgan Chase and Deloitte join the MoU network. TGIIC signs as technology partner — giving TechM4India access to Telangana's government enterprise networks and policy infrastructure.

2025

Six Lab Formats · N-STEP Live · TechM4Space Launch

All six deep-tech lab formats finalised and ready for installation. N-STEP first cohort launched — AI, Cybersecurity, FinTech, IoT, and Space tracks running simultaneously. TechM4Space announced as India's only EdTech aerospace program.

2026

University Partnerships · National Scale Begins

First formal university partnership negotiations underway. Anurag University in active discussion. Target: 10 partner institutions by end of 2026, 100,000 students touched within 3 years. The platform is ready. The scale begins now.
